Where did the name "PseudoCoin" come from?

The word pseudo means false, thus making us a "false"coin. This is true because crypto powered through the Binance Smart Chain network are refered to as tokens.

Will the PseudoCoin team Dox themselves?

We are planning to slowly DOX ourselves as our coin and team grows.

What are your marketing plans?

We are going to market through a variety of channels to reach as many potential community members and investors as possible. Some of our planned marketing methods include; Social media advertising and "social rushes," paid banner ads on websites such as PooCoin, influencer shoutouts and sponsorships, our own merchandise store, tracking websites such as CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap, audits, and exchange listings to list a few.

How do we plan to combat whales?

A 6% tax is implemented on every PseudoCoin transaction. The tax exists on one hand to discourage continuous rapid transactions by bots or whales looking to make a quick buck and on the other hand to incentivize investors by implementing a reward system for holding onto the token.
